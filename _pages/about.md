I'm a husband, father, and retired military veteran. I'm currently working as a security consultant, and enjoy doing TryHackMe and Hack The Box in my spare time. When I'm not doing that, I love cycling and beer tasting. 

I hold a Master of Science in Cybersecurity from Utica University, as well as a number of security-related certifications including the Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP), Practical Network Penetration Tester (PNPT), Offensive Security Certified Professional (OSCP), eLearnSecurity Certified Professional Pentester (eCPPT), and the eLearnSecurity Certified Web Pentester (eWPT).
